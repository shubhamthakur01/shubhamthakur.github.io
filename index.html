About Me

Greetings! I'm Shubham Thakur, a dedicated Data Scientist currently contributing to the innovative environment at Walmart Global Tech in Sunnyvale as part of the Personalization team. Our primary focus is constructing sophisticated, large-scale personalized recommender models using customer intent. My academic journey led me to a Master's degree in Data Science from the University of San Francisco (USF). I have over 3 years of experience working in this field. During my academic pursuit, I worked as a Data Science (NLP) Intern at LexisNexis, where I developed of Information Extraction frameworks. This initiative significantly enhanced the personalized recommendation of legal briefs. Prior to that, as an Analytics Consultant at EXL Analytics, I played a pivotal role in the Credit Risk domain. Our team successfully identified a loss exposure exceeding $10+ MM by implementing early fraud detection strategies in the Credit and Demand Deposit portfolio. This accomplishment earned me the recognition of Promising Newcomer.My technical proficiency includes Python programming, adept data retrieval and processing with SQL, and navigating distributed data systems using Pyspark and Hadoop. Venturing beyond academics, during my undergraduate years, I served as the Analytics Head of the Consulting and Analytics club at IIT Guwahati. We designed an introductory data science course for the first time to promote data science culture on the campus. Today, this course is lauded as the best free data science course by Analytics India Magazine. I remain actively engaged in mentoring aspiring students, helping them embark on successful careers in Data Science.
